About Bosele Kgotla VDC

Our Mission

To foster sustainable community development through participatory decision-making, preserving our cultural heritage while embracing progress for the betterment of all residents in our village.

Community Unity

We bring together community members from all walks of life to work collaboratively on projects that address local needs and improve our quality of life.

Sustainable Development

Our initiatives focus on long-term sustainability, ensuring that development projects benefit current and future generations while respecting our environment.

Our Key Activities

Water & Sanitation

Coordinating with relevant authorities to ensure clean water access and proper sanitation facilities for all community members.

Infrastructure Development

Advocating for and overseeing road maintenance, community center improvements, and other essential infrastructure projects.

Education Support

Supporting local schools, organizing adult literacy programs, and promoting educational opportunities for all ages.

Health Initiatives

Coordinating health awareness campaigns, supporting clinic operations, and promoting community wellness programs.

Agricultural Projects

Supporting local farmers, promoting sustainable farming practices, and coordinating community gardening initiatives.

Traditional Governance

Facilitating kgotla meetings, mediating disputes, and maintaining our traditional decision-making processes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Attend our monthly kgotla meetings on the first Saturday of each month. You can also contact any committee member to express your interest in volunteering for specific projects or initiatives.

Projects are funded through a combination of government grants, NGO partnerships, community contributions, and fundraising activities. All financial decisions are made transparently through kgotla consensus.

Traditional leadership works alongside elected committee members to ensure development respects our cultural values and follows proper consultation processes through the kgotla system.

Issues can be raised during kgotla meetings, reported to any committee member, or brought to the attention of the village headman. We encourage open communication and community participation.

Yes, all kgotla meetings are open to community members. We encourage active participation in discussions and decision-making processes that affect our village.
